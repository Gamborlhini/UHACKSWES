Record

Open the app, press the record button, and you are good to go. Nota will run in the background, recording your class, interview, or whatever else.

Review

Nota automatically converts the words it hears into text, so if you miss something, you can always look back and find it.

Refresh

After you are done recording, Nota will summarize the transcript into key terms. Making it easier to study and note-take.
